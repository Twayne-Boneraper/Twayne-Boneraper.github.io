Company Profile

Anhui Zhongbang Biological Engineering Co., Ltd. , as the factory of Anhui Zupont Import & Export Co. Ltd. ,was established in 1997.
It was jointly initiated and founded by Anhui Investment Group Co., Ltd., Anhui Development Investment Co., Ltd. and the Institute of Chemical Engineering & Metallurgy of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
It is a company dedicated to the research and development, and production of agrochemical.
The factory is a national high-tech enterprise and has passed ISO9001 international quality system certification, ISO14001 environmental management system and GB/28001 occupational health and safety management system certification.
The factory implements 5S management. The factory was moved to Anhui Dingyuan Salt Chemical Industrial Park( provincial ) in 2016. It covers an area of 7 hecatres and has complete production equipments.
It can produce a variety of formulation: WP, DP, DS, SG, WS, WDG, EC, SC, OD, ME, SL, ULV, FS,CS ETC.The company has been committed to the development of the domestic market,
and has registered more than 100 pesticide products in China and more than 200 brandnames for the domestic market, established extraordinary reputation, and occupies a certain market share.
Currently, we are expanding to the international markets, such as Southeast Asia, Africa, South America and Europe. The company has excellent teams not only in R & D, production/quality management but also in marketing, we will provide professional technical support and high-quality services to our customers.
